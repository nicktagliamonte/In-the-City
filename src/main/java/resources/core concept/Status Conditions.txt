* hunger
    * lower move speed
    * lower ability for stealth
    * lower ability in a fight (to hit, and lowered damage)
* thirst
    * diminished stamina
    * eventually, slow health drain
    * lowered perception (distracted by thirst)
* exhaustion
    * penalties to all actions 
    * amplify fear
    * reduced recovery
* fear
    * lowers effectiveness in perception, negotiation, avoidance of certain locations (some locations become unavailable in a fear state)
* terror
    * everything from fear but the only non-closed off options for movement are those that take you out of the current situation
