Optional Side Quest: Fleeting Memories
Objective: Help a lost soul find closure by confronting their memories.

Quest Overview: Players encounter a spectral figure who claims to have lost something important—a memory of a loved one. They must help this ghost retrace their steps, but the memories become increasingly distorted and deceptive.

Quest Steps:

The Illusive Search:

Players follow the ghost to various locations, only to find memories that don’t seem to belong to them. Each memory is filled with contradictions or misleading details that make players question what they know.
Gaslighting Element: As they collect fragments of the memory, players may discover clues that point towards different realities or truths, creating doubt about the ghost’s identity and intentions.
Reality Distortion:

In one area, players might relive a past encounter with the ghost, only to find that the events have changed. The ghost may appear as an ally, then shift to a figure representing their fears or failures.
Gaslighting Element: This psychological twist prompts players to rethink their previous encounters and how their choices have impacted their relationships with other characters.
The Final Revelation:

To help the ghost find peace, players must confront the last memory, which reveals the truth behind the ghost's fate. The area around them warps dramatically as they uncover the truth.  The ghost is a truly awful person and, as this is revealed, the ghost is watching you, not the memory.  you leave.
Gaslighting Element: The player’s understanding of reality will be tested; they may find that the true nature of the ghost conflicts with the memories they’ve gathered. This conflict creates an emotional resolution for the ghost, but leaves players with lingering questions about their perception of the past.
