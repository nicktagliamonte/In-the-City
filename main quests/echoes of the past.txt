Main Quest: Echoes of the Past
Objective: Navigate the spectral phenomena surrounding the Liacouras Walk bell tower to uncover hidden memories and the truth about the area’s history.

Quest Overview: As players explore the area, they will experience disorienting events and cryptic messages that challenge their perception of reality. The environment itself seems to play tricks on them, and they must distinguish between genuine threats and fabrications.

Quest Steps:

Haunting Whispers:

Players begin to hear whispers that seem to lead them in various directions. Sometimes, these whispers give hints about the next objective, while other times, they lead to traps or dead ends.
Gaslighting Element: The whispers may change or become contradictory, prompting players to question what is real. They might hear someone calling their name or referencing past choices in a way that suggests the game knows them intimately.
The Bell Tower’s Toll:

As players approach the bell tower, the tolling of the bell resonates with their character, causing them to relive a memory linked to their past (a failed mission, a lost ally, etc.). The tolling alters their perceptions, making shadows seem to flicker closer.
Gaslighting Element: After hearing the bell toll, players may briefly see visions of themselves in different contexts (e.g., as a hero, as a failure) or experience time distortions, leading to confusion about what they are experiencing.
The Library of Forgotten Knowledge:

Players must find a way into the spectral library, which contains ancient spells and lost memories. The ghostly librarians present puzzles that test the player's knowledge, but the questions seem to change based on the player's earlier answers.
Gaslighting Element: Occasionally, players will find notes or journal entries that seem to reference actions they haven’t taken or choices they haven't made, leaving them questioning their own decisions and reality.
Garden of Echoes:

In the Memory Garden, players encounter memories of past students. These memories can either aid or hinder the player, revealing clues or misleading them into believing they have missed critical information.
Gaslighting Element: Some memories will manifest as false images of loved ones or allies, leading players to act against their better judgment. After interacting with these memories, players may find that they have no lasting impact on the environment, reinforcing a sense of futility.
Confronting the Spirit:

At the quest's climax, players must confront a powerful spirit that embodies their deepest fears and regrets. This spirit can manipulate the environment and project visions that confuse the player.
Gaslighting Element: The spirit may reveal truths about the player’s past but twist them into accusations or falsehoods. As players attempt to fight or reason with the spirit, the ground may shift, altering the battlefield in ways that play tricks on their sense of reality.
