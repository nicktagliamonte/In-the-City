Quest Steps:

You can feel the weight of the air, thick and charged with unspent energy.

Navigate the Labyrinth:
    You take your first steps down the corridor, where the path splits. Each direction seems equally foreboding. Choosing one path leads you into a room that echoes with your footsteps, but as you turn, you find yourself back at the entrance, as if the hall is playing tricks on your perception.
        You step into the corridor to the left, but the walls seem to ripple like water. Suddenly, you're back at the entrance, the corridor twisting back on itself, mocking your choice. The air grows colder, and the whispers grow louder, urging you to reconsider your path.

Solve the Cyclical Puzzle:
You must discern the patterns in the labyrinth. Shadows flicker, revealing symbols on the walls that appear and disappear, offering cryptic hints. You realize that each room reflects a piece of a larger puzzle—a series of choices leading to your final ascent or descent.

Symbol Selection: 
You encounter three symbols: a spiral, a tower, and a pit. The choice you make will dictate your next steps. Observing the patterns in the shadows provides clues about which path to take next.

With every step, the atmosphere warps further—sounds become echoes, and the walls bleed into one another. You begin to realize that you are not just climbing up; you are also delving deeper into the core of the loop.

Conclusion:
