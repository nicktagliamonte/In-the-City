Determine What Each Merchant Wants:

Merchant A has the water purification tablet (essential item) but will only trade it for food rations and one additional item of low value.

Trade Sequence:

Step 1: Start with Merchant B.

The player can trade their piece of technology for Merchant B's fuel canister.
Step 2: Next, go to Merchant A.

Now, the player has food rations and the fuel canister. They can give these two items to Merchant A in exchange for the water purification tablet.
Step 3: Finally, go to Merchant C.

The player still has medical supplies in their inventory and now also has the water purification tablet (a high-value item in the ruins).
