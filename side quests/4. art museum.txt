Optional Side Quest: A Shattered Memory
Objective: Help a fellow survivor recover a lost memory within the shifting halls.

Quest Overview: In the main gallery, the player meets Liana, a woman searching for a locket she claims to have lost “in a dream.” She explains that she last saw it in one of the museum’s shifting rooms, but the layout has since changed. Liana is deeply troubled, as though finding the locket will help her remember something important.

Quest Steps:

Speak with Liana: The player learns about her vague, dreamlike memory of the locket, her lost keepsake. She offers a portion of her supplies if the player agrees to search.
Explore the Museum for Clues: As the player solves other puzzles, they occasionally encounter distorted echoes of Liana’s memories—fleeting sounds or visions showing a young woman exploring the museum.
Find the Locket: The player finally locates the locket in a small, almost invisible compartment within the Room of Mirrored Echoes. Returning it to Liana reveals an unexpected twist: she has no memory of the locket or the player, as if she has been caught in the museum’s strange influence herself.
Outcome:

Reward: If the player returns the locket, they receive a small stash of useful supplies. The encounter leaves them with the unnerving impression that the museum alters not just space, but memory.
Atmospheric Impact: Liana’s fragmented memory and lack of recall hint at the museum’s power, reinforcing the idea that this space erodes both time and perception.
