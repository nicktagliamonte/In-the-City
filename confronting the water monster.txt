Main Quest: Confronting the Water Monster
Objective: Gather resources while avoiding or confronting the Water Monster in the depths of the Metalwoods.

Quest Overview: Players must prepare for a dangerous expedition into the Metalwoods, where they’ll collect rare resources while evading the Water Monster. The quest emphasizes strategic planning and careful exploration, as players must decide when to confront the beast or avoid it entirely.

Quest Steps:

Enter the Metalwoods:
Upon entering the park, players are immediately struck by the dense underbrush and murky waters. The environment is filled with mutated flora that can be both beneficial and harmful.

Gathering Supplies:
Players must search for unique plants and resources scattered throughout the park. Some may provide buffs or healing, while others could cause harm if consumed.

The First Encounter:
While exploring, players have their first encounter with the Water Monster. It lurks beneath the surface, creating ripples in the murky water. Players must decide whether to flee or try to set a trap using their gathered resources.

Strategic Planning:
Players can return to the safe zone at the remnants of the picnic area to rest and strategize. Here, they can trade with NPCs for valuable items or hints on dealing with the Water Monster.

Final Confrontation:
Players must return to the depths of the Metalwoods, using traps and resources effectively to confront the Water Monster in its lair. They can choose to confront it directly or attempt to navigate around it to gather the final resources needed for their quest.
