Items
1. resource items
    * base: water
        * low scarcity
        * no barter value
        * weight: 1 unit
    * common: scrap material
        * Description: Random, salvaged parts from machines (e.g., screws, metal shards, old wires, wood).
        * Uses: Repairs, minor crafting, simple weapons, basic traps, and barter with lower-tier NPCs.
        * Scarcity Level: Low.
        * Barter Value: 1 unit.
        * Weight: 1 unit
    * moderate: food rations
        * Description: Preserved and easily portable food items (canned goods, dried meats, grains).
        * Uses: Essential for survival and health regeneration; high trade value in all zones.
        * Scarcity Level: Moderate; limited to specific zones and encounters.
        * Barter Value: 5 units
        * weight: 2 units
    * uncommon: medical supplies
        * Description: Basic first aid, bandages, antiseptics, pain relievers. Essential for recovery and minor healing.
        * Uses: Restores health, crucial for sustaining longer exploration and surviving encounters.
        * Scarcity Level: Uncommon; frequently found in economic zones or near settlements but rare in hostile areas.
        * Barter Value: 10 units.
        * weight: 3 units
    * Rare: Fuel and Energy Cells
        * Description: Old fuel canisters, batteries, or specialized cells needed for running machinery and transportation.
        * Uses: Powers conveyance, lights, or small machinery; has high barter value with specialized NPCs.
        * Scarcity Level: Rare; found in high-danger zones or from specific NPCs in exchanges.
        * Barter Value: 25 units.
        * weight: 4 units
    * Very Rare: Ancient Relics
        * Description: Unique and mysterious items from before the collapse (e.g., an old watch, rare jewelry, strange technological artifacts).
        * Uses: Mostly valuable for bartering, unlocking certain story elements, or as powerful talismans.
        * Scarcity Level: Very rare; found only in hidden or high-risk zones.
        * Barter Value: 50 units.
        * weight: 5 units
2. crafting supplies
    * Description: A versatile, compact toolkit containing essential materials and tools for crafting and repairing various items. The kit includes wires, circuit boards, adhesive, and an assortment of small mechanical parts, all neatly organized in a rugged, weather-resistant case. The case is adorned with symbols and instructions that only a skilled Technologist can fully appreciate.
    * Use: This toolkit allows the Technologist class to craft new items, upgrade existing gear, or repair damaged equipment. It can be used at any crafting station or during downtime in safe zones to create unique gadgets or modify weapons. When equipped, the toolkit provides a crafting efficiency bonus, reducing the resource costs for crafting by a small percentage.
    * Weight: 2 units
    * First encountered: stage 3
3. lockingpicking tools
    * Description: A finely crafted set of lockpicking tools housed in a durable leather pouch. The kit includes various picks, tension wrenches, and rakes, each designed for different lock types. The tools are sturdy and posess a weight beyond what it seems they should, showing signs of frequent use and expert craftsmanship, with a technological underpinning that could account for their frequent success. The pouch also has compartments for small spare parts and a small flashlight for dark environments.
    * Use: This toolkit allows the Survivalist class to pick locks on doors, chests, and other secure locations throughout the game world. Successfully using these tools can grant access to hidden areas, rare resources, or shortcuts. When equipped, the lockpicking tools increase the chances of a successful pick, providing a bonus to skill checks related to lockpicking.
    * Weight: 3 units
    * First encountered: stage 3
4. cryptic amulet
    * Description: An ornate amulet crafted from an iridescent stone that seems to shift colors in the light. Its surface is etched with cryptic symbols and surrounded by a delicate chain. When worn, the amulet emits a faint, soothing hum, almost like a whisper, which calms those around the wearer.
    * Use: This amulet is the special item for the Negotiator class, enhancing their ability to communicate and persuade. When equipped, it grants bonuses to charisma and negotiation rolls, allowing the wearer to defuse tense situations or sway opinions in their favor. Additionally, the Whispering Amulet can be used once per day to cast a charm effect, temporarily boosting the goodwill of NPCs towards the wearer, making them more receptive to offers or requests.
    * weight: 2 units
    * First encountered: stage 3
